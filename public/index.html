Privacy Policy

Data Collection

WebApps Hub is designed with privacy in mind. All applications work locally in your browser and do not collect or transmit personal data to external servers.

Some applications may use your browser's local storage to save your work and preferences. This data remains on your device and is not shared with us or third parties.

We may use privacy-focused analytics to understand how our applications are used, but this data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users.
